Frequently asked questions

What is global payroll software?

Global payroll software processes employee and contractor payments across multiple countries, handling local tax calculations, compliance requirements, currency conversions, and statutory benefits automatically.

What is the difference between EOR and global payroll?

An Employer of Record (EOR) acts as the legal employer in countries where you don't have a legal entity, handling all compliance. Global payroll software processes payments but requires you to have local entities. Most modern platforms offer both.

How much does global payroll cost?

EOR services cost $199-$599/employee/month. Contractor management runs $29-$49/contractor/month. Enterprise global payroll aggregators use custom pricing based on headcount and countries.

Which global payroll software is easiest to use?

Deel and Multiplier consistently earn the highest ease-of-use scores. Both offer self-serve onboarding and can have your first international employee set up within days.
