Frequently asked questions

What is HRIS software?

HRIS (Human Resource Information System) software is a database system that manages employee information — personal records, job history, time off, performance reviews, and org charts — in one place.

What is the difference between HRIS, HRMS, and HCM?

HRIS focuses on employee data management. HRMS (HR Management System) adds process automation like payroll and benefits. HCM (Human Capital Management) is the broadest category, covering talent management, workforce planning, and analytics.

Do I need an HRIS if I already have payroll software?

Yes — payroll software handles payment processing but lacks HR features like org charts, performance reviews, onboarding workflows, and document management. Most companies use both together.

What is the best free HRIS?

Zoho People offers the most generous free plan (up to 5 users). Freshteam also has a free plan for up to 50 employees. Both cover core HRIS needs without a payroll module.
