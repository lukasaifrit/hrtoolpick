Last updated: July 14, 2026  ·  Effective immediately

Short version: HRToolPick earns a commission when you click our links and sign up for a paid plan. This never affects our ratings, rankings, or editorial opinions — our comparisons are based on verified data, not who pays us.

What is an affiliate relationship?

HRToolPick participates in affiliate marketing programs. This means that when you click on certain links on our website and subsequently make a purchase or sign up for a service, we may receive a commission or referral fee from the company at no additional cost to you.

Affiliate links are typically identified by buttons labeled "Try [Tool Name]" or similar calls to action on our comparison pages.

How does this affect our content?

It doesn't — and here's why we're confident saying that:

  • Our ratings are sourced from verified third-party platforms (G2, Capterra) and are not influenced by affiliate relationships.
  • We compare tools on objective criteria: features, pricing, supported countries, integrations, and user ratings.
  • We include tools in our database regardless of whether they have an affiliate program. Several tools we review (including Paycom, Workday, and Justworks) do not pay us any commission.
  • Our verdicts and "Who should use each tool" sections are generated based on data, not commercial relationships.

FTC compliance

In accordance with the Federal Trade Commission's guidelines on endorsements and testimonials (16 CFR Part 255), and similar regulations in the UK (ASA/CAP), Canada (Competition Act), and Australia (ACCC), we are required to disclose our affiliate relationships clearly and conspicuously.

We believe in full transparency. This disclosure page, combined with the affiliate disclosure note present on every comparison page, satisfies our legal obligations and our commitment to honest publishing.

Pricing and data accuracy

We make every effort to keep pricing, feature lists, and ratings accurate and up to date. However, software pricing and features change frequently. We recommend always verifying current pricing directly on each vendor's official website before making a purchasing decision.
