Frequently Asked Questions

Is QuickBooks Payroll better than Employment Hero?

It depends on your needs. QuickBooks Payroll is generally better for small business, QuickBooks users, while Employment Hero shines for Australian businesses, UK businesses. Both have strong ratings on G2 and Capterra.

How much does QuickBooks Payroll cost compared to Employment Hero?

$45/mo + $6/employee vs $8/mo + $8/employee. Always check each vendor's official pricing page as rates may have changed.

Can I switch from QuickBooks Payroll to Employment Hero?

Yes, both platforms support data export. Most businesses complete a migration in 2–4 weeks. It's best to run both in parallel for one payroll cycle before fully switching.

Does QuickBooks Payroll or Employment Hero offer a free trial?

Yes, QuickBooks Payroll offers a free trial. Yes, Employment Hero offers a free trial.

Which is better for international payroll — QuickBooks Payroll or Employment Hero?

Neither currently offers international payroll — consider Deel or Remote for global hiring.
