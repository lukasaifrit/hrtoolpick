Frequently Asked Questions

Is BambooHR better than Square Payroll?

It depends on your needs. BambooHR is generally better for small business, medium business, while Square Payroll shines for small business, retail. Both have strong ratings on G2 and Capterra.

How much does BambooHR cost compared to Square Payroll?

$10/mo + $10/employee vs $35/mo + $6/employee. Always check each vendor's official pricing page as rates may have changed.

Can I switch from BambooHR to Square Payroll?

Yes, both platforms support data export. Most businesses complete a migration in 2–4 weeks. It's best to run both in parallel for one payroll cycle before fully switching.

Does BambooHR or Square Payroll offer a free trial?

Yes, BambooHR offers a free trial. Yes, Square Payroll offers a free trial.

Which is better for international payroll — BambooHR or Square Payroll?

Neither currently offers international payroll — consider Deel or Remote for global hiring.
